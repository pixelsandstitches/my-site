Typography
Display — Fraunces (Google Fonts · SIL open source)
Pixels & Stitches
hand-crafted, pixel by pixel
Fraunces is an old-style serif with a built-in "wonk" variable axis — its letters carry intentional imperfection that closely mirrors Millie June's hand-drawn character. Use for logos, hero text, pull quotes, and section headers. The italic is especially expressive.
Body + UI — Zilla Slab (Google Fonts · SIL open source)
Every pattern begins with a single stitch, and every pixel begins with a single point. These are the small decisions that build something worth keeping.
Zilla Slab is a slab serif with a hand-constructed, slightly irregular texture — the closest Google Fonts analog to the Grocery Font's artisanal feel. Works at every size: body copy, navigation, product tags, and captions.
Utility / Labels — Zilla Slab 600, tracked
New Pattern · Digital Download · Maker's Kit
Same family as body, heavier weight + wide tracking. Covers badges, tags, nav items, and CTAs. No need for a third family.
